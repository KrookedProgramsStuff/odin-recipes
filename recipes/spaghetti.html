Krooked's Spaghetti Sauce

Ingredients

  • 1 Quart Chicken Stock
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 lb ground sausage
  • 2 6oz cans tomato paste
  • 2 bay leaves
  • Lots of garlic (at least half a head)
  • 2-3 celery stalks finely chopped
  • 1 medium white onion finely chopped
  • 1 medium green bell pepper finely chopped
  • 1/2 tbsp rosemary
  • 1/2 tbsp thyme
  • 1/4 tbsp allspice
  • 1/4 tbsp nutmeg
  • 1 handful dry basil
  • 2 tbsp chopped parsley
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 jar mushrooms
